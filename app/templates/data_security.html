APPERTIVO

Data Security

Your recipes, menus, and concepts stay locked inside your workspace. Appertivo combines infrastructure safeguards with strict access controls to protect your data.

Built on modern, audited cloud infrastructure

Platform Security

Appertivo runs on hardened cloud environments with automatic patching and monitoring.

  • Encrypted storage for every asset you upload or generate.
  • TLS 1.2+ enforced for all data in transit between your browser and our APIs.
  • Continuous monitoring with alerting for suspicious activity.

Access Controls

Only the right people can reach your workspace and data.

  • Role-based permissions within Appertivo to limit who can view, edit, or export content.
  • Strict internal policies ensure employee access is granted only for support or maintenance.
  • Audit trails covering key actions inside the product.

Data Retention & Backups

We keep your data available while giving you control over removal.

  • Regular backups with encryption at rest and isolated storage.
  • Workspace owners can delete dishes, menus, or entire accounts at any time.
  • Deleted data is purged from active systems within 30 days unless legally required otherwise.

For vendor questionnaires or deeper security reviews, email security@appertivo.com. We’re happy to walk through our controls.
