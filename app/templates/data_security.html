Data Security

Your recipes, menus, and concepts stay locked inside your workspace. We can't view or reuse them. Appertivo only draws on information that is already public on the internet, and there is currently no way to send us additional proprietary data.

Built on modern, audited cloud infrastructure

How Appertivo Protects Your Work

Appertivo runs on hardened cloud infrastructure with automatic patching, encrypted storage for every asset, TLS 1.2+ enforced for data in transit, and continuous monitoring with alerting for suspicious activity.

Role-based permissions give you control over who can view, edit, or export content inside your workspace, while strict internal policies ensure Appertivo employees only receive access when you request support. Workspace owners can delete dishes, menus, or entire accounts whenever needed, and removals are purged from active systems within 30 days unless legal requirements say otherwise.

Need vendor documentation or a deeper security review? Email security@appertivo.com and our team will help.
