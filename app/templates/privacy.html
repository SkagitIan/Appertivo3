APPERTIVO

Privacy Policy

We treat every menu, idea, and review you share with Appertivo as confidential. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have.

Last updated January 2025

Information We Collect

Everything we collect is directly related to helping you generate better dish concepts and run your workspace.

  • Account and team details you provide when creating or inviting users.
  • Menus, concepts, ratings, and prompts created within Appertivo.
  • Product usage signals that help us keep the platform reliable and secure.

How We Use Your Data

Appertivo only uses your data to power experiences in your workspace and to improve functionality for your team.

  • Training and tuning AI suggestions specifically for your restaurant.
  • Delivering features like favorites, menu collections, and collaboration tools.
  • Monitoring system stability and preventing abuse or unauthorized access.

We never sell, trade, or share your culinary concepts outside of your account. Period.

What We Do Not Collect

Appertivo never pulls point-of-sale data, customer records, or supplier contracts. We only work with information you intentionally create inside the product.

Your Controls

  • Export or delete dishes, menus, and concepts whenever you choose.
  • Request full account removal by emailing privacy@appertivo.com.
  • Reach support any time for clarification on how we handle information.
