Privacy Policy

We take the privacy of every restaurant seriously. This policy outlines how Appertivo handles the information created inside the platform and what we never do with your data.

What we collect

  • Account and team details you provide when signing up.
  • Concepts, dishes, and favorites created by your team in Appertivo.
  • Usage metrics that help us keep the service reliable and secure.

How we use it

We use your in-product activity to personalize your workspace and improve our AI suggestions for your restaurant only.

We never sell, trade, or share your concepts, dishes, or prompts outside of your account.

What we do not collect

Appertivo does not ingest point-of-sale records, supplier information, or customer data. We only work with what you create inside our tools.

Your controls

  • Export or delete concepts, dishes, and menus at any time.
  • Request full account removal by emailing privacy@appertivo.com.
  • Contact support for clarification on any data handling question.
