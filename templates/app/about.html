About Appertivo

Restaurants live and die by their specials. A dish that shines today, a happy hour that pulls in tomorrow’s regulars—these moments create momentum. But too often, the tools to share them are clunky, scattered, or just built for someone else.

Appertivo was built to change that.

From Skagit County, Washington, we set out to make it effortless for restaurants to snap a photo, add a few details, and instantly publish their specials everywhere that matters—Google, email, social media, and their own website.

No juggling five platforms. No wasted staff time. No flying blind on what actually worked. With Appertivo, every special gets the reach it deserves, every owner gets back a little breathing room, and every diner gets a reason to show up today.

Why we do it

Because restaurants aren’t just businesses—they’re anchors for community. We believe their stories, dishes, and daily creativity should travel farther and faster than word-of-mouth alone.

How we do it

We build with Django, Python, and a clean design philosophy: zero friction, maximum automation. Behind the scenes, AI helps polish descriptions, track performance, and keep things running smooth. Up front, restaurant owners stay in control, without needing to be tech experts.

Appertivo is not about replacing human craft—it’s about amplifying it.
